### ActionStopSounds

ActionStopSounds instructs Flash Player to stop playing all sounds.

| Field            | Type               | Comment           |
|------------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| ActionStopSounds | ACTIONRECORDHEADER | ActionCode = 0x09 |
