### ActionPlay

ActionPlay instructs Flash Player to start playing at the current frame.

| Field           | Type               | Comment           |
|-----------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| ActionPlay      | ACTIONRECORDHEADER | ActionCode = 0x06 |
