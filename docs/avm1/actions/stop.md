### ActionStop

ActionStop instructs Flash Player to stop playing the file at the current frame.

| Field           | Type               | Comment           |
|-----------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| ActionStop      | ACTIONRECORDHEADER | ActionCode = 0x07 |
